01 · About

The story behind the dashboards

I'm a BCA Graduate specializing in Artificial Intelligence & Data Analytics at LNCT University, Bhopal — motivated by one simple idea: data only matters when someone can act on it.

My work sits at the intersection of business questions and messy spreadsheets. I clean the data, model it, and package the answer into a dashboard, query, or report that a decision-maker can actually use. Along the way I've analyzed customer sentiment, tracked sales trends, modeled an online bookstore's database, and built an HR analytics dashboard end-to-end.

During my internship at Indolike, I worked hands-on with Python, NumPy and Excel to clean, transform and visualize real datasets — turning raw exports into trends stakeholders could actually discuss. I'm continuing to sharpen my SQL, Power BI and prompt-engineering workflow, using AI tools like ChatGPT, Copilot and Claude to move faster without cutting corners on rigor.

Career objective — to contribute technical skill, adaptability and a continuous-learning mindset to a data-driven organization, while growing into a well-rounded Business Intelligence professional.
